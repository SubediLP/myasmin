Why Choose Koshi?

Koshi is a college noted for creative learning and innovative ideas with a culture and environment necessary for personality development.

Location

Our college stands at the key location in Itahari-1 within just a few minute walk from a city center.Students in the immediate neighbourhood can go to collge on foot. For other students there are easy motorable road accesses to college.

Academic Excellence

Our college is accliamed for quality classroom delivery & student-orinted research works & their presentations. Perhaps this is the reason that we have been Purbanchal Topper for three times and Provience 1 topper for a time

Facilities

The college has a modern computer lab and three other labs for Physics, Chemistry & Biology. A huge library with several thousand books related to diverse areas of knowledge and a spacious seminar hall are further attractions. In addition, we have a power-generator for 24-hour power supply. Clean & cool drinking water is made available for all students.

Project-based

Our teaching is mostly project-based. Students work hard here putting together the ideas they learn and making something out of these. This not only strengthens their concept-building & visualizations but also immenesly enhances their life skills.Every year Koshi organizes a mega-exhibition for students to display their project works.

Scholarships

We have a number of scholarships schemes for gifted students.We grant Koshi Ambassardorship Award to ten KAA exam toppers. We also grant special cash award & full/partial scholarships to engtarnce test toppers,term exam toppers and to students from marginalised communities.

Transportation

We are in possession of 9 college buses. Students form Inaruwa, Duhabi, Laxmi Marga, Tarahara, Tal-talaiya & Kalabanjar come to college on the buses everyday enjoying the sight of the beautiful cityscape..
